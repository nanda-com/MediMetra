Report Collection

Upload medical reports via PDF or Image from your gallery or camera. Keep all your health history in one secure place.

AI Summarizer

Our offline AI extracts structured info about patients, doctors, and medicines from your uploaded reports instantly.

AI Assistant (RAG)

Get answers about your health records and general medical questions using an offline LLM running data-privately.

Medicine Reminders

Stay on track with local notifications for your medications, directly scheduled from your extracted report data.

QR Code Sharing

Generate time‑limited QR codes for doctors to scan and view your health summary securely without sharing raw files.

Offline First

Ensures data privacy and works without reliable internet, using open‑source LLMs on Google Colab for core AI processing.

System Architecture

MediMetra leverages a hybrid architecture combining Flutter, Firebase, and a custom AI backend on Google Colab.

  • 🔹 Frontend: Flutter Mobile App with Hive for local storage.
  • 🔹 Auth & Database: Firebase Auth, Firestore, and Cloud Functions.
  • 🔹 AI Backend: FastAPI, Llama 3 8B (via Ollama), and PaddleOCR.
  • 🔹 Search: ChromaDB and Multilingual e5-small embeddings.
